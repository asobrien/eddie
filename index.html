EDDIE ARE YOU OK? SO, EDDIE ARE YOU OK? ARE YOU OK EDDIE?

Actually, no, Eddie’s not. It so happens that Michael Jackson’s song Smooth Criminal is not, as is widely misinterpreted, about the eponymous Eddie. In fact, the song refers to Annie, a woman attacked in her apartment by a smooth assailant. So the question is: Annie, are you OK?

So what is this site about anyways?

In one respect this is either a gift to a friend, an inside joke, or a thank you card—although I’m not sure which. Yet, it also a site that showcases the power of community, openness, sharing, and the generosity of others.

STRENGTH IN FELLOWSHIP

Given these gifts, a little CSS and a bunch of HTML, I’m able to post my digital graffiti on wall of the web—all the while leveraging the wonderful work of others.

The typography is cool!

I’d have to agree. Well, maybe there is an overload of typefaces here but we’ll leave that to the critics. The layout is provided by the use of a very neat JavaScript plugin Hatchshow by Charlie Park. The typefaces used are all in the Google web fonts library. The following type faces are used throughout: Candal, Diplomata, Bowlby One SC, Ultra, Holtwood One SC, Playfair Display, Rye, Sancreek, Asset, and Ewert. Determining which is which is left as an exercise for the reader.

Why did you spend $8–15 to register such a ridiculous domain name?

I didn’t. This is a just a subdomain of ruok.org, one of the many free TLDs provided by the free DNS service afraid.org. I haven’t the faintest idea of what ruok.org is, or if it is even serving up content—how could one pass up the opportunity to add eddie as a subdomain given the chance?

Where is this site hosted?

Next to my printer. From a Raspberry Pi. With nginx. Statically.
